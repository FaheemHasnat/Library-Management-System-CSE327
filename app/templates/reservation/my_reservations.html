How Book Reservations Work

1. Search & Reserve

Search for books and select multiple books to reserve at once. You can reserve available books or join the queue for issued books.

2. Wait for Notification

You'll be notified when your reserved books become available for collection.

3. Collect Your Books

When books show "Ready to Collect", visit the library within 24 hours to collect them.

4. Cancel if Needed

You can cancel active reservations anytime if you no longer need the book.
